> **How to find your technic log**
> **--------------------------------------**
> 1. Go into the **Launcher Options** (upper-right corner)
> 2. On the bottom-left corner of that screen, click **"Open Logs"**
> 3. The logs are all called **techniclauncher_YYYY-MM-DD.log**, with YYYY being the Year, MM being the Month, and DD being the day. **We need the Logs from when you were/are having your issues**.
> 4. Send the logs into **chat**
